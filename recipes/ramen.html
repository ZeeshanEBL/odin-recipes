Ramen Noodles

Description

We will be making a traditional bowl of Shoyu Ramen. A bowl perfect for those that prefer a not so heavy bowl of ramen for those chilly nights. The added Chili paste will help with that too!

Ingredients

  • Stock of your choice. (Beef, Chicken, or Pork)
  • Ramen Noodles
  • Chili Paste
  • Diced green onions
  • Sliced Pork Belly

Steps

  1. Heat up chosen stock by pooring into a pot and bring over heat until hot
  2. Cook noodles by placing into a pot of boiling water and leaving in for 5 minutes
  3. Poor stock into a bowl followed by the noodles
  4. Garnish with green onions off to a side, a teaspoon of chili paste on top, and sliced pork belly to one side

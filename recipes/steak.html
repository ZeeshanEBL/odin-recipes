Steak

Description

Tender and delicious Ribeye Steak cooked to a juicy perfection! Seasoned with fresh herbs, then cooked in a cast-iron skillet and basted with garlic butter, everyone will love this easy steak recipe! My Rib Eye Steak recipe is an easy Valentine's Day dinner idea, and comes together in just 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Steak cut of your choice
  • Grapeseed oil or other oil that tolerates high heat
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Pat steak dry with a paper towel and season both sides liberally with salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan on high heat and add oil. Swirl pan so oil is evenly spread
  3. Lay steak away from you and sear all sides. Allow steak to release naturally do not pull
  4. Once all sides are seared lower heat and add butter and herbs
  5. Baste steak until cooked to your preferance and set on plate. Allow to rest for 5-10 minutes before serving
